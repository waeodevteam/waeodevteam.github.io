What is Waeo?

Waeo is an innovative playlist synthesizing interface for the connected generation.

When you can access your favorite music individually on various personal devices and online resources - why should your current playlist be based on a single repository?

With Waeo, you can build playlists that integrate music from disparate local & online sources, thereby making for an unshackled listening experience. Host a playlist on your wi-fi, connect from any device of your choice and build as you go!

Jazz up your soirees with Waeo's social spin - allow your friends to add songs of their own and vote on the playlist to make things interesting!
